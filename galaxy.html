I love your eyes. If you think about it, they're kind of like galaxies. I find myself captivated by your eyes. I wish I could see them and get to know what's behind them. Not just the most beautiful parts of them, but also their flaws. I want to be the person you celebrate with during your triumphs, as well as the person you feel comfortable sharing your moments of insecurities, vulnerabilities, and weaknesses with. You have one of the purest hearts out there, and you don't deserve to shoulder everything on your own. Now that I'm around, I won't let you.
