Happy Valentine's Day, My Love

Though there are miles separating us at this moment, I still hold you very close to my heart. In such a short amount of time, you've already become one of the people I treasure most in life. Every moment I spend with you is a memory worth cherishing forever.

It was a long journey before we found each other, but I honestly wouldn't have it any other way. I genuinely do believe you are the one. You are the most wonderful person I've ever had the privilege of knowing, and I hope one day you see that too. You deserve all the love in this universe—more than what even I can give. I pray you never give up on me, your dreams, and yourself. I am so proud of how far you've come, and I'm so excited for us to further grow into better people together.

To be loved by you is the best thing that's ever happened to me, and to love you is the best thing I've ever done. My love for you is boundless.

I'm not a religious person, but I do sometimes think God made you for me.

From the bottom of my heart,

Nela
